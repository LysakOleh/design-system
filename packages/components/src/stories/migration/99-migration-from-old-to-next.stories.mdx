# Migration from old to next

The following workflow walks through the steps of migrating.
Note that the actual steps required for your project may vary, and these steps should be treated as general guidance rather than strict instructions.

## Card

The interface of the card is the same, however the usage of the typography changed.
The first `bal-heading` has now the level `h5` and the subtitle is changed into a simple paragraph.

```html
<!-- old -->
<bal-card class="p-5">
  <bal-heading level="h4" space="none">BaloiseCombi</bal-heading>
  <bal-heading subtitle level="h5" space="bottom" color="info">Police number 70/2.937.458</bal-heading>
  <p>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna
    aliqua.
  </p>
  <bal-button-group position="right" class="mt-5">
    <bal-button color="info" outlined>Secondary Action</bal-button>
    <bal-button>Main Action</bal-button>
  </bal-button-group>
</bal-card>

<!-- new -->
<bal-card class="p-5">
  <bal-heading level="h5" space="none">BaloiseCombi</bal-heading>
  <p>Police number 70/2.937.458</p>
  <p>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna
    aliqua.
  </p>
  <bal-button-group position="right" class="mt-5">
    <bal-button color="info" outlined>Secondary Action</bal-button>
    <bal-button>Main Action</bal-button>
  </bal-button-group>
</bal-card>
```
